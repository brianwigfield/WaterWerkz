How it works

Everybody is flocking to, or at a minimum investigating these newer 'Agile' methods to delievering software with promises of fixing their woes with 'traditional' waterfall processes. Common issues mentioned by the critics are that you can't guarantee how long software will take to develop or that it will get completed on an 'arbitrary' date because of the nature/complexity of software.

Agilists will be quick to point out that doing agile practices allow you to manage software risk. We think that switching to a different process is not the best option to manage your risks. Not unlike managing other risks in life we offer insurance for your waterfall software project. The exact forumla we use is a closely guarded business secret that we have designed after man years of measuring variances in other software projects.

Once we engage with you, we will assign a emissary to keep tabs on your managers and developers to see how the project is progressing, in addition we will manage your change management process for optimum accountability. If at some point we feel the project is falling behind or scope has crept too much then we will bring in our crack team of developers to bring you back up to a point in the project we feel comfortable with. If for some reason the project does not deliever the intended value, included in the cost of our service is insurance premiums to reimburse the lost business value.
